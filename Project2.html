Project Overview

This project showcases the development of innovative solutions and highlights its key outcomes.

Category: Mobile Application Design, UI/UX Design
Language & Tools Used: Figma
Year Published: 2024
Description: This is a wireframe prototype of what my Goblet & Grub restaurant app could look like if designed for mobile, created through Figma. Through this project, I've learned how to craft an immersive user experience by focusing on the details—such as the rich use of colors and interactive elements—that bring the medieval fantasy theme to life. I've also prioritized an intuitive user interface, ensuring that users can easily navigate through the enchanted dining experience, from booking a table to customizing their feast in a seamless, enjoyable way.
